average energy, valence, and danceability of those songs.
Energy is a measure of how fast and loud a song sounds,
valence is a measure of the positivity or negativity of
the song's emotional content, and danceability is a measure of
how suitable a song is for dancing based on its rhythm, tempo, and beat.
